Jan - Jun 2019, Sep 2019 - Jun 2020, Sep 2020 - present

<h2 id="acc">Instructor, <i>UCLA DCISS</i></h2>

Jun - Jul 2016, Oct - Nov 2017

At the [UCLA Dashew Center for International Students and Scholars (DCISS)](https://www.internationalcenter.ucla.edu/), I instructed the course, [American Culture & Conversation (AC&C)](https://www.internationalcenter.ucla.edu/programs-events/language-development#acc-instructors). The goal of the course is to help international students acculturate to America and improve their confidence with conversational English. As an instrcutor, I prepared a syllabus and weekly lesson plans, presentations, in-class interactive activities, and homework assignments.

<h2 id="elc">English Language Circle Leader, <i>UCLA DCISS</i></h2>

Jan - Jun 2018

<h2 id="doep">President, Member, <i>UCLA DOEP</i></h2>

April 2013 - Jun 2015

While attending UCLA for undergrad, I directed the UCLA student-run organization, [Drug Outreach & Education Program (DOEP)](https://doep67.wixsite.com/doep), whose mission is to inform the LA community on the physiological and psychological effects of drug use and abuse. My responsibilities included organizing events and public presentations for community centers, organizations, and schools; communicating with service recipients; managing and applying for organization funding; preparing and running weekly meetings; transporting members and supplies to presentation venues; and managing other officers.

<h2 id="special-o">Community Service Commission (CSC) Liaison, Coach, <i>UCLA Special O</i></h2>

Sep 2013 - Jun 2015

While attending UCLA for undergrad, I served as the CSC Liaison and was a coach at the UCLA student-run organization, <strong><u>Special O</u></strong>, that aims to provide a healthy environment for intellectually disabled adults to improve their sports skills and build self-confidence. Here, I acted as an intermediary to facilitate communication between our organization and the overseeing community service body (CSC) and transported food and equipment for the athletes. In addition, I coached intellectually disabled adults from the Greater Los Angeles area in various sports on a weekly basis by helping set up drills and run practices
